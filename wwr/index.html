What We Do,

Whitewater rafting is a type of recreation that involves navigating a non-motorized watercraft down free flowing rivers. A rafting company organizes trips, provides the watercraft, life jackets, etc., and typically offers experienced guides to take passengers down a river. The business intent is to provide a thrilling and safe experience to individuals and groups for a fee.

What Do We Offer?

We offer a range of different rafting trips. There are many different access point along the Colorado River. Each with its own unique layout. We have trips that involve hiking to hot springs or up mountain trails. We offer two day trips that camp over night on the river. We offer weekend getaways for large parties. Food is provided on all trips longer than 6 hours.
